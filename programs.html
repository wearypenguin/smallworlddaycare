Our Programs

Age-appropriate learning from infancy through pre-kindergarten

Classrooms Designed for Growth

Each of our 10 classrooms is thoughtfully designed to meet the developmental needs of children at different stages. Our curriculum promotes intellectual curiosity, social-emotional growth, and school readiness through age-appropriate activities and experienced educators.

Infants

6 weeks - 12 months

Our infant program provides a nurturing, home-like environment where your baby receives individualized attention. We follow your child's schedule for feeding, sleeping, and play while promoting early development through sensory activities, language development, and motor skill building.

  • Low teacher-to-child ratios
  • Individual care plans following your baby's routine
  • Daily communication via ProCare Connect app
  • Safe, stimulating environment

Voluntary Pre-Kindergarten (VPK)

4 - 5 years

We are a State of Florida VPK approved provider. Our VPK program prepares children for kindergarten success through a comprehensive curriculum aligned with Florida's Early Learning Standards.

  • Free for Florida residents (VPK scholarship)
  • Focus on literacy and mathematics
  • Social skills and emotional regulation
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving
  • School readiness assessments
  • Kindergarten preparation

Our Approach to Learning

Play-Based Learning

Children learn best through play. Our curriculum incorporates hands-on activities that engage curiosity and foster a love of learning.

Individualized Attention

We recognize that each child develops at their own pace. Our teachers provide individualized support to meet each child's needs.

Family Partnership

We believe parents are a child's first teacher. We maintain open communication and welcome family involvement.
