Afterschool Program

School Age | Fun, safe care from 2:30 PM - 6:00 PM plus summer camp

Our Afterschool Program

Welcome to our afterschool program, where school-age children find a safe, supportive environment to decompress after a full day of learning, get homework help, and engage in enriching activities with friends. We provide the perfect balance of structure and freedom, academic support and creative play.

Our experienced staff creates a relaxed yet engaging atmosphere where children can be themselves, explore their interests, build friendships, and develop new skills. We partner with local schools to provide transportation (where available) and work closely with families to ensure each child's afterschool time is productive and enjoyable.

Summer Camp Program

Looking for summer care? Our summer camp program provides full-day care with exciting weekly themes, special activities, field trips, and plenty of outdoor fun. Children enjoy a structured yet relaxed summer experience with friends.

Summer Camp Highlights:

  • Full-day programming
  • Weekly themed activities
  • Field trips and excursions
  • Water play and outdoor fun
  • Arts, sports, and STEM activities
  • Guest presenters
  • Special events and celebrations
  • New friendships and memories
