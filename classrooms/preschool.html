Our Preschool Program

Welcome to our preschool classroom, where learning comes alive through hands-on exploration, creative discovery, and meaningful play! Our preschool program provides a rich curriculum that balances academic skill development with social-emotional growth, preparing children for the transition to kindergarten.

At this age, children are naturally curious, asking "why" and "how" about everything around them. Our experienced teachers channel this curiosity into engaging activities that build literacy, math, science, and social studies concepts while fostering critical thinking, problem-solving, and a genuine love of learning.

Sample Daily Routine

Note: Our structured routine provides consistency while allowing flexibility for teachable moments.

Literacy activities, letter/sound work, writing practice
