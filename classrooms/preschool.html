Preschool

3 - 4 years

A Place For Activity

Our specific goals for the pre-school program are to provide for every child a comfortable transition from the home environment to the school atmosphere. We also hope to offer each child an exciting yet challenging year of growth as a total person. All areas of a child's development are considered: social, emotional, physical, cognitive, perceptual-motor and creative. In order to accomplish these goals, our pre-school curriculum seeks to balance the program equally between social-effective growth and cognitive development.

This curriculum must be strongly experimental to provide learning opportunities in all areas that actively involve the young child in the learning processes. At the beginning of the school year the emphasis is on large group experiences with intermittent small group instruction and free choice activities. As the child nears completion of the preschool experience, he/she begins to encounter a more structured day in order to become fully prepared to enter pre-kindergarten.
