Toddlers

12 - 24 months | Supporting independence and exploration

Our Toddler Program

Welcome to our toddler classroom, where walking, talking, and exploring are celebrated every day! This exciting stage is filled with rapid development as your child becomes more mobile, curious, and independent. Our experienced teachers provide a safe, engaging environment that encourages exploration while maintaining consistent routines and positive guidance.

We understand that toddlers are learning to assert their independence while still needing lots of support and comfort. Our program balances freedom with structure, offering plenty of opportunities for active play, creative expression, and social interaction with peers.

What Makes Our Toddler Room Special

Care & Environment

  • Low 1:6 toddler-to-teacher ratio
  • Experienced toddler specialists
  • Safe, toddler-proofed classroom
  • Separate nap area with cots
  • Child-height furniture and learning centers
  • Secure outdoor play area

Development & Communication

  • Daily reports via ProCare Connect app
  • Photos and milestone tracking
  • Meal, nap, and activity documentation
  • Positive behavior guidance
  • Individualized attention and support
  • Parent-teacher communication
