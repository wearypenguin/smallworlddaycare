Small World Daycare is a State of Florida approved VPK provider. Florida's Voluntary Prekindergarten (VPK) program is completely FREE for all eligible 4-year-olds who live in Florida. This program prepares children for success in kindergarten and beyond.

Our VPK Program

Welcome to our VPK classroom, where kindergarten readiness is our primary focus! Our VPK program follows Florida's Early Learning and Developmental Standards, ensuring your child receives high-quality instruction aligned with state requirements. Through engaging, hands-on activities, we build the academic, social, and emotional skills needed for kindergarten success.

As a state-approved VPK provider, we conduct regular assessments three times per year to track your child's progress in literacy, mathematics, and social-emotional development. Our experienced, qualified teachers create a structured yet playful learning environment that fosters confidence, curiosity, and a lifelong love of learning.

VPK Eligibility & Enrollment

Who is Eligible?

  • Children who turn 4 years old by September 1st
  • Florida residents
  • Must apply through Florida's VPK portal

How to Enroll:

  1. Apply for VPK certificate at Florida VPK website
  2. Receive your VPK certificate
  3. Contact us to reserve your spot
  4. Submit certificate and enrollment forms
