Our Infant Program

Welcome to our infant classroom, where we provide a warm, nurturing environment that feels like a home away from home. We understand that leaving your baby in someone else's care is a significant decision, and we are committed to earning your trust through exceptional care and constant communication.

Our experienced caregivers follow your baby's individual schedule for feeding, sleeping, and playtime. We believe in providing consistent, loving care that supports each infant's unique developmental timeline while offering plenty of opportunities for growth and exploration.

What Makes Our Infant Room Special

Care & Safety

  • Low 4:1 infant-to-caregiver ratio
  • Experienced infant care specialists
  • Safe, clean, developmentally appropriate environment
  • Separate sleeping area with cribs for each baby
  • Secure diaper changing station
  • Bottle preparation and warming area

Communication

  • Daily reports via ProCare Connect app
  • Photos and videos throughout the day
  • Feeding, diaper, and nap tracking
  • Milestone documentation
  • Open communication with caregivers
  • Parent conferences as needed
