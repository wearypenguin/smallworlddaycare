Two's

2 - 3 years

A Place For Everyone

No matter what color their skin is, what type of clothes they wear, if a child comes from different family structures, or what part of the world they may come from, we teach children to respect and cherish people from all backgrounds and walks of life.

While we do focus on structure and education our mission is to also try to teach our children things that we feel are missing from the world today, which to us are to be friends with empathy, compassion, and true caring and understanding for each other.
