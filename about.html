About Us

Over 20 years of quality early childhood education

Our Story

Small World Daycare & Learning Center opened its doors in 2002, taking over the location from an existing child care facility, Small World Nursery & School. This facility has provided childcare for over 40 years. Our facility is purpose-built as a childcare center with 10 classrooms, offices, and a cafeteria.

We are proud to be a nationally accredited child care facility, committed to providing the highest quality early childhood education in a safe, nurturing environment.
