About Us

Over 20 years of quality early childhood education

Our Story

Small World Daycare & Learning Center opened its doors in 2002, taking over the location from an existing child care facility, Small World Nursery & School. This facility has seen a childcare in place for over 40 years. This facility is purpose-built as a childcare facility with 10 classrooms, offices, and a cafeteria.

Hours of Operation

We are open Monday to Friday from 6:30am to 6:00pm.
