Frequently Asked Questions

What are your hours of operation?

We are open Monday through Friday from 6:30 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on major holidays.

What ages do you serve?

We serve children from 6 weeks through pre-kindergarten (approximately 5 years old). We have age-appropriate classrooms for each developmental stage.

Do you accept the Early Learning Coalition subsidy?

Yes! We are an approved provider for the Early Learning Coalition of Alachua County. Contact us for more information about applying for subsidies.

Are you a VPK provider?

Yes, we are a State of Florida approved VPK provider. Florida's Voluntary Prekindergarten program is free for eligible 4-year-olds.

How can I schedule a tour?

We'd love to show you around! Call us at (352) 376-0917 to schedule a convenient time for your visit.

Do you provide meals?

Yes, we provide nutritious meals and snacks throughout the day. We accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies with advance notice.

Have Questions?

We're here to help! Contact us with any questions about enrollment, programs, or our facility.
